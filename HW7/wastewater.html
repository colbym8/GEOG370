Homework 7: Travis County Wastewater Connectors

Colby Mask

The map depicts the total number of wastewater service connectors per square mile
in Travis County (Austin), Texas. The ratio was created using count data of total
wastewater service connectors divided by the land area from the data sources below.
The boundaries are the different zip codes within Travis County.

The wastewater service connector density reflects the Austin, TX population density
map included at the bottom of the page. This map is included to demonstrate the
need for infrastructure density to match population density, especially in urban
areas like Austin, TX, a city of over 1 million people.

Map uses a UTM Zone 14N Projection
