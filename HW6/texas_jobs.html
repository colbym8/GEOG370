Homework 6: Choropleth Map

Colby Mask

The map depicts the total number of employed people within the 20-24 age group in
each Texas county. Five of the ten most populous counties in the state are referenced
in the map. The scale was created to more evenly distribute the counties across all five
ranges. The range maximum values increase at a factor of 10 to emphasize the significant
separation of employment numbers between different counties.

Map uses a UTM Zone 14N Projection
